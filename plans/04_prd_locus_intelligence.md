# PRD: Locus Intelligence (Executive Dashboard)

## Overview
Locus Intelligence is the strategic half of the platform, designed exclusively for Executive Pastors (Dr. Robert). It relies on the clean data generated by Locus Core to provide high-level, predictive insights.

## Scope
This PRD governs the read-only, strategic intelligence dashboard.

### Core Features
- **Predictive Attrition (Drift Report):** Identifies individuals or families whose engagement patterns suggest they are drifting away.
- **Burnout Risk:** Identifies key volunteers who are over-scheduled or showing signs of fatigue.
- **Bus Factor Graph:** Analyzes volunteer dependency to identify single points of failure in ministry operations.
- **Retention Funnel:** Tracks how effectively newcomers are integrated into the life of the church over time.

## UI/UX Mandate: "Boardroom Ready"
- The interface must be clean, serious, and professional.
- **Absolute Prohibition:** There must be zero gamification in this workspace. No confetti, no avatars, no streaks, no "Speed Run" timers.
- The data should be easily exportable or presentable for executive meetings.
- All visualizations (charts, graphs) should prioritize clarity and actionable insight over flashy animations.
